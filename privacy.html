Dev Toolkit Extension — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Data Collection

Dev Toolkit does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All data stays on your device.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API (chrome.storage.sync) to save your favorites and recently used tools. This data is synced across your Chrome browsers via your Google account, but is never sent to our servers.

No Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. There are no cookies.

No Network Requests

The extension does not make any network requests. When you click a tool, Chrome opens it in a new tab — that's a standard browser navigation, not a request from the extension.

Permissions

The extension requests only the storage permission, used to save your favorites and recent tools. No other permissions are requested.

Third-Party Services

The tools linked by this extension are hosted on GitHub Pages (yurukusa.github.io). Each tool's own privacy characteristics apply when you open it.

Contact

For questions, open an issue at github.com/yurukusa/dev-toolkit-ext.
